Professional Background

As a student of architecture, I embarked on an entrepreneurial venture, independently undertaking design projects for residential and commercial spaces. Subsequently, I joined the "Instituto Metropolitano de Planificación – IMP," a government entity, where I conducted comprehensive analyses of San Borja, Lima, Peru. Collaborating with a team of professionals, we formulated and presented urban planning solutions to address the city's challenges. Following this experience, I contributed my skills to "Tektum Construcción y Arquitectura SAC," a prominent architecture studio. Within the design sector, I leveraged my analytical abilities to understand client requirements and deliver optimal proposals. Later, at "WAO – World Architecture Office," my responsibilities extended to the construction sector. In a supervisory role, I demonstrated leadership skills, gaining insights into sector-specific processes. Throughout my professional journey, I diversified my experience by engaging in various roles, including sales and customer service in call centers, pet store operations, restaurant dishwashing, warehouse management, and roles as a fast packer, barista, and bartender. My work ethic is deeply oriented towards achieving the goals of the organizations I serve, consistently surpassing expectations with favorable outcomes.
